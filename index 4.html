About Us

Established in 1980, Insurvey India is an ISO 17025:1999 NABL-accredited laboratory offering mechanical, chemical, metallurgical, and civil quality assurance services. Serving over 1200 clients including multinational corporations and public sector units, our team of experts ensures reliable, globally recognized testing solutions.

Our Core Services

  • Destructive Testing (Tensile, Impact, Hardness, Metallurgical Analysis)
  • Non-Destructive Testing (Ultrasonic, Radiography, Eddy Current, MPI, etc.)
  • Engineering Services (Risk-Based Inspection, Residual Life Assessment, Fitness for Service)
  • Consultancy Services (QA Lab Setup, ISO Certification Support)
  • Training & Certification (NDT Levels, AWS-CWI, API Exams)

Testing Capabilities

  • Mechanical & Metallurgical: Yield Strength, Elongation, Grain Size, Microstructure
  • Chemical: Elemental Analysis by Spectrometry and Wet Methods
  • Materials Tested: Metals, Alloys, Plastics, Polymers, Rubber, Construction Materials, Textile
  • Special Methods: Thermography, Boroscope, Acoustic Eye, Magnetic Flux Leakage
