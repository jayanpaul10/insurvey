Destructive Testing Services

1.METALS & ALLOYS (STEEL PLATES, STRIPS, FLATS, SECTIONS, AND BARS), CAST & FORGED ITEMS OF BOTH FERROUS AND NON-FERROUS ITEMS.

  • A.PHYSICAL TESTS

    • Yield Stress, UTS, Elongation & Reduction of Area, Bend test, Hardness (Rockwell, Brinell & Vickers), Charpy Impact.

  • B.METALLURGICAL TEST

    • Microstructure, Grain Size, Rate of inclusion with and without Photomicrograph, Depth of Decarburization, Case Depth and Thickness of Electroplating.

  • C.GALVANISATION TEST

    • Mass of Coating, Thickness of Coating, Uniformity of Coating, and Adhesion test.

  • D.CHEMICAL TEST (BY SPECTRO AS WELL AS BY WET ANALYSIS)

    • C, Mn, Si, S, P, Ni, Cr, Mo, V, Al, Cu, Nb & Ti.

2. HIGH-STRENGTH DEFORMED STEEL BARS & WIRES FOR

  • A.PHYSICAL TESTS

    • 0.2% Proof Stress, U.T.S, Elongation, Weight per meter Run, Surface Characteristics, Bend and Rebend Test

  • B. CHEMICAL TESTS

    • C, S & P

3. FASTENERS

BOLTS & RIVETS

  • A.PHYSICAL TESTS

    • For full-size Bolts: Tensile, Hardness, Wedge tensile, Head soundness, Proof Load test, Shear Strength & cantilever test. For machined specimen Yield strength 0.2% proof Stress, UTS and elongation, Dump test of Rivets, Torque test of bolts. N.B. : All the above testing arrangements are available with a maximum load of 60 tons for the sizes. 1. (M3 to M39) 2. (1/4”,3/8”,1/2”,5/8”,3/4”,7/8”,1: BSW) 3. (1/2”, 5/8”,3/4””,1”)

  • B.METALLURGICAL TEST

    • Surface integrity, Decarburisation, Retempering, and micro test (carbide precipitation for S.S. bolt), Embrittlement test, and surface discontinuities.

  • C.GALVANISATION TEST

    • Mass of coating, Uniformity of Coating, verification of coating, coating thickness, and adhesion of the coating.

  • D.CHEMICAL TEST (BY SPECTRO AS WELL AS BY WET ANALYSIS)

    • C, Mn, S & P – for Carbon Steel Bolts and C, Mn, Si, Ni, Cr, Mo, S & P – for Alloy Steel Bolts.

Nuts

  • A.PHYSICAL TESTS

    • Proof load test, Hardness, Widening Test, Drift test, Ageing Test & Torque Test. [Sizes of the nuts are the same as mentioned in bolts with a maximum load of 60 tons].

  • B.METALLURGICAL TEST

    • Surface discontinuities, Embrittlement test, and carbide precipitation for S.S. Nuts.

  • C.GALVANISATION TEST

    • Mass of coating, uniformity of coating, Adhesion, verification of coating & thickness of the coating.

  • D.CHEMICAL TEST (BY SPECTRO AS WELL AS BY WET ANALYSIS)

    • C, S & P – for Carbon Steel Nuts and C, Mn, Si, Ni, Cr, Mo, S & P – for Alloy Steel Nuts.

SPRING WASHER

  • A.PHYSICAL

    • Permanent set test, Permanent load test, Twist test, and Hardness test.

  • B.GALVANISATION TEST

    • Mass of coating, Uniformity of coating, Thickness of coating, and verification of Coating.

4. WELDED PLATE & PIPES

  • A.WPQ & PQR TEST

    • Transverse Root Bend Test, Transverse Face Bend, Transverse Side Bend Test, Transverse Tensile Test. All weld tensile tests, Longitudiual Bend tests, Hardness Surveys & Fracture Tests.

  • B.METALLURGICAL

    • Macro test, I.G.C. Test (Practice A, B, & E as per ASTM A 262)

  • C.CHEMICAL TEST

    • Parent Metal as well as weld metal.

5. STAINLESS STEEL SHEET, STRIP, BARS, AND OTHER PRODUCTS

  • MECHANICAL TEST

    • Microexamination with carbide precipitation and Intergranular test as per ASTM A-262, Practice – A,

  • B.METALLURGICAL

    • Macro test, I.G.C. Test (Practice A, B, & E as per ASTM A 262)

  • C.CHEMICAL TEST

    • C, Si, Mn, S, P, Ni, Cr, Mo, V, Cu, Ti, and Cb. (BY SPECTRO AS WELL AS WET METHOD)

6. OTHER STEEL PRODUCTS

  • A.MECHANICAL TEST

    • Load Compression test of Spring, a Load test of U-Bolt & Shackles. Flattening test of Tubes / Pipes. Expansion / Drifting test of Tube. Wrapping test of Wire.

7. NON-FERROUS METAL

  • A.MECHANICAL TEST

    • Proof Stress, UTS, and Elongation. Hardness – Brinell (62.5 kg, 250 kg, and 500 kg load) and Vickers (1 kg, 5 kg, and 10 kg load)

  • B.CHEMICAL TEST

    • Copper and Copper Alloys: Cu, Ni, P, Zn, Pb, Fe, Al, As, Bi, Si, and Sn. (BY WET ANALYSIS) Aluminium and Aluminium Alloys: Si, Cu, Pb, Fe, Mg, Ni, Sn, Mn, Ti, Cr & Zn. (BY SPECTRO AS WELL AS BY WET ANALYSIS) Nickel and Nickel Base Alloys: Complete analysis. Ferro Alloys: Complete analysis except for ferrochrome.

8. HEAT TREATMENT OF METAL

Heat Treatment is done when the tensile test is specified after heat treatment. Heat Treatment Schedule is provided for required Coupon Hardness and Vickers (1 kg, 5 kg, and 10 kg load)

9. PLASTIC AND POLYMERS

Polymer Products, Fiber Reinforced Plastics, Flexible and Rigid Foam, Molded Polymer Components, Plastic Films and Sheets, Plastic Materials Products, and Plastic Pipes.

  • Tensile Strength & Elongation
  • Compressive Strength, Shore Hardness D
  • Cross breaking, Bending strength

    • 10. Rubber and Rubber Products

    Gaskets, Seals and different types of Packings, Industrial Belts, Natural and Synthetic Rubber Products, Molded and Extruded Rubber Sheets for testing of Compounding Tyres, and many Misc. Products.

  • Tensile, Modulus, Hardness Shore A
  • Compression & Tension set
  • Angular Tear, Adhesion to Fabric/Metal
  • Breaking strength for belts

    • 11. Textile Cotton Woolen Fabric

    Weight per unit area, Ends & Picks, Count of yarn, shrinkage/elongation, Breaking strength, scouring loss, starch/sizing material, weaving, wool content, cotton content, nylon content, polyester content, rayon content, viscose content of the blended fabric, bundesmann test, barium content/degree of mercerization and composition of blended fabric.

    12. Construction Materials

  • Cement
  • FINE AGGREGATE & COARSE AGGREGATE (SAND & STONE CHIPS)

    • Aggregate abrasion value, crushing value, impact value, grading & fineness modulus, flaking index, elongation index, specific gravity, bulk density, water absorption, soundness, aggregate alkali, reactivity, deleterious material, bulking of sand and silt content in fine aggregate.

  • BUILDING BRICKS

    • Crushing strength (compressive strength), water absorption, efflorescence & dimensions.

  • CERAMIC UNGLAZED VITREOUS TILES

    • Squareness, warpage, water absorption, compressive strength, flexural strength (up to 60 Ton) & resistance to acid.

  • BITUMEN FELT

    • Breaking strength, pliability, heat resistance test, pressure leakage test, and water absorption.

  • PAVING BITUMEN

    • Specific Gravity, Water Content, Flash Point, Softening Point, Penetration, Penetration Ration, Ductility, Matter Soluble in Trichloroethylene, Viscosity.

  • BLOWN BITUMEN

    • Specific Gravity, Flash Point, Penetration, Ductility, Matter Soluble in Trichloroethylene.

  • BENTONITE

    • Moisture Content, pH, Sieve Analysis, Marsh Viscosity, Liquid Limit, Plastic Limit, Free Swell.

  • PLASTICISER

    • Physical and chemical tests.

    13. Wrapping Coating Material

  • COALTAR TAPE

    • Thickness, weight, breaking strength, adhesion as per AIWWA: C-203-97 solvent insoluble/soluble content. Test the coaltar component in the tape (softening point, penetration, and filler content).

  • PAINT PRIMER TYPE–B

    • Type of the paint, drying time, flash point, volatile matter, viscosity, coverage, and application property

  • COALTAR HARD PITCH

    • Softening point, specific gravity, ash content, trichloroethylene soluble and petrol insoluble content.
