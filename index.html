About Us

Insurvey is a leading Inspection and Survey organization offering a wide range of quality verification services in the field of Mechanical, Metallurgical, Chemical and Civil Engineering since 1980. Accredited by NABL under ISO 17025:1999, Insurvey delivers globally recognized testing solutions.

  • Non-Destructive Testing (Ultrasonic, Radiographic, Eddy Current, Thermography, etc.)
  • Engineering Services (RBI, RLA, FFS, Failure Investigations)
  • Consultancy Services (QA Lab Setup, ISO Certifications)
  • Training Programs (NDT, AWS, API Certifications)
